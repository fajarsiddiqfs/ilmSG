About Qiblah
The Qiblah is the direction toward the Masjid al-Haram in Makkah. Muslims face the Qiblah during the five daily prayers. The golden needle indicates the direction of the Kaabah from your current location. Move your device in a figure-8 motion to calibrate the sensor if the reading seems inaccurate.

Daily Duas
Authentic supplications from Quran & Sunnah
Morning
أَصْبَحْنَا وَأَصْبَحَ الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
Asbahna wa asbahal mulku lillah, wal hamdu lillah
We have reached the morning and at this very time unto Allah belongs all sovereignty, and all praise is for Allah.
Sahih Muslim 2723
Evening
أَمْسَيْنَا وَأَمْسَى الْمُلْكُ لِلَّهِ، وَالْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ
Amsayna wa amsal mulku lillah, wal hamdu lillah
We have reached the evening and at this very time unto Allah belongs all sovereignty, and all praise is for Allah.
Sahih Muslim 2723
Before Sleep

Bismika Allahumma amutu wa ahya
In Your name, O Allah, I die and I live.
Sahih Bukhari 6324
After Waking
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ الَّذِي أَحْيَانَا بَعْدَ مَا أَمَاتَنَا وَإِلَيْهِ النُّشُورُ
Alhamdulillahil-ladhi ahyana ba'da ma amatana wa ilayhin-nushur
Praise be to Allah who gave us life after having taken it from us and unto Him is the resurrection.
Sahih Bukhari 6312
Eating

Sahih Bukhari 5376
Entering Home
بِسْمِ اللهِ وَلَجْنَا، وَبِسْمِ اللهِ خَرَجْنَا، وَعَلَى اللهِ رَبِّنَا تَوَكَّلْنَا
Bismillahi walajna, wa bismillahi kharajna, wa 'ala Allahi rabbina tawakkalna
In the name of Allah we enter, in the name of Allah we leave, and upon our Lord we place our trust.
Abu Dawud 5096
Travel
سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ وَإِنَّا إِلَى رَبِّنَا لَمُنْقَلِبُونَ
Subhanalladhi sakhkhara lana hadha wa ma kunna lahu muqrinin, wa inna ila Rabbina lamunqalibun
Glory be to the One Who has subjected this to us, and we could never have [otherwise] subdued it. And indeed, to our Lord we will return.
Quran 43:13–14
Distress
